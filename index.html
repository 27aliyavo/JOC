Book Review

And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie is an amazing murder mystery. Set on an island, a group of strangers meet in a mansion. Slowly, they all get murdered. The book follows them as they try to figure out why they are getting murdered, and by who. The book has a lot of good twists, and is a very fun read.

Movie Review

Oceans 8 is the sequel of the other Oceans' movies, and is in mu opinon one of the best ones. It's about a conwoman who gets released from prison, and then recruits a group of women to rob the Met. It is very fun and exciting to watch, and it's really interesting to see how they plan out the robbery.
